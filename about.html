Our Story

Building a tradition of excellence and holistic growth since 2015.

Who We Are

Froebel School Anantnag was established with a singular vision: to create a learning environment where academic excellence goes hand-in-hand with activity-driven development. Located in Gulshan Abad, K.P Road, we have become a trusted institution for parents who seek a modern, balanced education for their children.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every child is unique and deserves a personalized approach to learning. By integrating arts, sports, and science into our daily curriculum, we ensure that students are not just studying, but growing.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of educational excellence in Anantnag, where every child is empowered to reach their full potential through activity-driven learning and character building.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and high-quality learning environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and a sense of responsibility in every student.

Message from our Principal

"Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think."

At Froebel Anantnag, we believe in creating an environment where students feel safe to take risks, ask questions, and explore their interests. Our curriculum is designed to balance rigorous academics with activity-based learning and strong moral character (Tarbiyat).

We invite you to visit our campus and see firsthand the energy, enthusiasm, and values that define the Froebel experience.

- Prof. Abdul Rashid Dar
